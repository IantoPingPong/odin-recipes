Bara brith

Description

A dark, rich fruit cake made in Wales

Ingredients

  • 450g dried mixed fruit
  • 250g brown sugar
  • 300ml warm black tea
  • 2 tsp mixed spice
  • 450g self raising flour
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Soak fruit and sugar in strined tea and leave overnight
  2. The next day, mix the remaining ingredients into fruit mix and beat well
  3. Pour mixture into a 2lb loaf tin
  4. Bake in pre-heated oven (170C) for 90mins
