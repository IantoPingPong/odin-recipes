Lasagna

Description

A delicious dish, well loved by Italians

Pasta inerleaved with bolgnese and cheese sauces

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Beef mince
  • Grated Cheese, flour and 500ml milk
  • Onions, Garlic and Italian Herbs
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Fry the onions and garlic
  2. Fry the Beef mince and once brown add in the onion garlic mix
  3. Add the tomato sauce and simmer
  4. Use the flour, milk and grated cheese to make a cheese sause
  5. Add a layer of the bolgnese sauce to the base of a deep dish
  6. Add a layer of Past sheets
  7. Add a layer of cheese sauce
  8. Repeat the layers until the dish is filled, finishing with a layer of cheese sauce
  9. Top with a layer of Cheese sauce
  10. Repeat the layering until the dish is full, culminating with a Cheese layer
  11. Bake the dish in the oven at 180C for 40 mins
